About Chawla

Established in the year 1976, with over 40 years of experience, Chawla Architectural and Engineering Consultancy has completed and executed a wide range of projects in the region. We build commercial, industrial and living spaces that are aesthetically pleasing and scientifically astute.

We have completed projects in all areas of Dubai and parts of other Emirates too. As we have an unlimited license we can conceptualize, build and supervise any kind of project. Over our 40+ years in the market we have successfully completed various high rise towers, hospitals, schools, villas, and communities.
